Good Old-Fashioned Pancakes

Description

Dakota Kelly found this pancake recipe in her Grandma's recipe book. Judging from the weathered look of this recipe card, this was a family favorite.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Baking Powder
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Milk and Butter
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Sift the dry ingredients together.
  2. Make a well, then add the wet ingredients. Stir to combine.
  3. Scoop the batter onto a hot griddle or pan.
  4. Cook for two to three minutes, then flip.
  5. Continue cooking until brown on both sides.
